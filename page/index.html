Overview
Recently youtube announced that adblockers no longer work on their platform, so i came up with a sollution that should make everyone happy. Yes, you heard me, the viewers won't have to watch annoying ads and creators will still gain money for watching ad.
How creators gain money from watching ads
The youtube give a creator their money from ads based on how many people watched WHOLE ad, this extension makes sure that the viewer wont see a single second of ad but trick the youtube to think that he watched whole.
Download
Downloading is pretty straightforward, just download this zip (there are all the necessary files for extension to work)
Next, unzip the file. You should be left with the folder with a few files
Next, open chrome, press 3 dot icon on the right-top of chrome window, then follow Extensions>Manage Extensions, new tab should open
Lastly, click the 'Load unpacked' button on top-left corner of the page and select the previously unpacked folder
That's it! You can now watch youtube without ads!
